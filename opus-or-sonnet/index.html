    § 05 / Sources & caveats

    What this page is and isn't

    1. Plan pricing: claude.com/pricing. Pro $17/mo annual ($20 monthly), Max from $100/mo, Team $20/seat standard ($25 monthly) and $100/seat premium, Enterprise $20/seat + usage.
    2. Model availability: All paid plans include Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku. Free includes Sonnet only per Anthropic's current plan sheet.
    3. Usage multipliers (Max 5x/20x, Team premium 5x standard) are Anthropic's published relational figures. Exact message counts vary by model and task and are not officially pinned.
    4. Performance bars are relative, calibrated from Anthropic's own positioning of each model. Not independent benchmark scores.
    5. The calculator is directional. It assumes a rational user won't exceed their plan's soft caps. Real-world pricing for Enterprise is negotiated.
    6. Last verified against Anthropic's public pricing page in April 2026. Plans and prices change; if you're making a procurement decision, check the source.
